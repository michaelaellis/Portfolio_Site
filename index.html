Game & Level Designer


My name is Michael Ellis and I'm a Full Sail Alumni with a Master's in game design.

During my studies at Full Sail, I had the opportunity to gain real world experience by completing my capstone with CelleC Games. With this opportunity I gained experience in Game Design, Level Design, Scripting, and Production. I am passionate about game design, not only because making games is fun and challenging, but also games bring a smile to people's faces. Fully immersing yourself into a world only conceivable by imagination and becoming someone else for a little while...that is why I'm passionate about game design. Crafting levels, balancing stats, or writing game design documents I always go above and beyond.

Enough about me...I am sure you want to see what I have done.

Go ahead, scroll. You know you want to!

Game Design & Quality Assurance

For Explorer Society I had the task of entering the questions and answers into the database object we created for the project. I must have played this game for hours to ensure each question and answer were correctly paired and were located in the correct country.

  • Input questions and answer for 192 countries
  • Provided QA for overall game playability
